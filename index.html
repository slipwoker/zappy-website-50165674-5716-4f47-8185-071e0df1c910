T-N-T Publishing bridges the literary and musical worlds, offering a unified home for creators who refuse to be confined to a single medium. From manuscript to master recording, we shepherd your work with editorial precision and strategic vision.

Book Publishing

Comprehensive editorial development, copy editing, and proofreading that refines your manuscript. We handle cover design, interior layout, and distribution strategy to position your book for kindle book publishing and print audiences alike.

ATV Music Publishing

Production guidance, mixing oversight, and sonic branding for recording artists. Our atv music publishing support extends to concordia music publishing standards, helping you navigate rights, licensing, and audience-facing presentation.
