Where Literature
Meets Sound

T-N-T Publishing Company operates at the intersection of storytelling and sound, cultivating a catalog that unites literary craft with musical vision. We are a dual-focus publishing house built for authors, songwriters, and creative professionals who demand work shaped with taste, precision, and deep audience awareness.

From Kindle book publishing to ASCAP music publishing and Concordia music publishing partnerships, our editorial process treats every project as a cultural artifact—whether it lands on a page, a playlist, or both.

A House Built on Sound & Story

T-N-T Publishing was founded on a simple belief: that the rhythm of a sentence and the melody of a song come from the same creative source.

In an industry that often asks creators to choose between the written word and the musical note, we saw an opportunity to build a bridge. Our dual-focus publishing house was born from the conviction that literature and music are not separate disciplines, but two expressions of the same human impulse: to tell stories that resonate.

Whether we are guiding an author through the journey of kindle book publishing or helping a songwriter navigate the complexities of atv music publishing, our approach remains steadfastly personal. We treat every manuscript and every master recording as a cultural artifact deserving of meticulous care.

Our team understands that publishing is both cultural stewardship and contemporary audience connection. We don't just put work into the world; we amplify it. From navigating concordia music publishing rights to crafting immersive literary experiences, our role is to ensure your voice finds its audience without compromise.

We believe that every creator deserves a partner who sees the full picture—someone who understands that a book can have a soundtrack and a song can have a narrative arc. At T-N-T, we're not just publishing content; we're curating a legacy of sound and story.

Our Core Values

Guided by artistic integrity, disciplined craft, and cross-medium collaboration, we bridge the literary and musical worlds to publish works that resonate deeply and endure.

Artistic Integrity

We believe every manuscript and composition deserves to be published with its creative vision intact. Our editorial process preserves the author's voice while refining the work to its fullest potential, ensuring authenticity from first note to final page.

Disciplined Craft

Publishing excellence demands rigorous attention to detail. From developmental editing and music engraving to cover design and production, we apply meticulous craftsmanship at every stage, treating each project as a bespoke artifact worthy of its audience.

Cross-Medium Collaboration

We operate at the intersection of storytelling and sound, fostering partnerships between authors, composers, and visual artists. This collaborative ecosystem produces richer, more immersive publications that transcend traditional boundaries and reach diverse audiences.
